ABOUT US

Founded in 2004 in downtown Jakarta, WatchHGoods was founded by a group of watch business people who wanted to bring a sophisticated watch shopping experience to the country.


While widely available across the country through more than 40 stores, WatchHGoods is stepping into the digital world to better serve its customers in a very busy and fast-paced world.
